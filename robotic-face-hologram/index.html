About this demo

Loads a photorealistic head scan and transforms it into a holographic android: chrome metal skin, custom scanline shader, glowing robotic eyes, and energy seams across the face panels.

  • Chrome android material — not human skin tones
  • Glowing robotic eyes with cursor tracking
  • Panel seams, temple ports, forehead HUD strip
  • Scanline + fresnel hologram shader on the mesh
