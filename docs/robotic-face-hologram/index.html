About this demo

Uses forced-perspective (anamorphic) display: the canvas is treated as a physical screen window. The camera projection is warped to match your eye position, so the android head appears to float in real space ~8 cm in front of the glass.

  • Wireframe mesh on by default — robotic scan look
  • Off-axis forced-perspective projection
  • Mouse or webcam head tracking
  • Cyan screen-frame marks the display plane (z = 0)
